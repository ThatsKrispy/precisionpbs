Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 1, 2026  |  Precision Paint & Body Shop · PrecisionPBS.Com

Our Commitment

Precision Paint & Body Shop is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Target ADA Compliant

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following built-in accessibility features:

Skip Navigation A "Skip to main content" link at the top for keyboard users
Keyboard Navigation Full keyboard accessibility with visible focus indicators throughout
ARIA Labels Semantic HTML and ARIA attributes for screen reader compatibility
Image Alt Text All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text
Color Contrast Text colors meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratio requirements
Responsive Design Fully functional on all screen sizes including mobile and tablet

Accessibility Widget

Our site includes a built-in Accessibility Widget (the blue button in the bottom-right corner of every page). It allows you to customize your experience with the following options:

  • Text Size — Increase or decrease font size using the A+/A– controls
  • High Contrast — Increases color contrast throughout the site
  • Large Text — Applies a globally larger font size preset
  • Reduce Motion — Disables scroll animations and transitions
  • Dyslexia-Friendly Font — Switches to a typeface and spacing optimized for readability
  • Underline All Links — Makes all hyperlinks visually distinct with underlines

Your preferences are saved locally in your browser and persist across visits.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some limitations may exist:

  • The embedded Google Maps iframe has limited accessibility beyond what Google provides natively. Directions are also available by phone or the text link below the map.
  • Third-party social media links (Instagram, Facebook) are operated by their respective platforms and subject to their own accessibility policies.

We are actively working to address all known issues.

Standards & Conformance

This website targets conformance with:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
  • ADA Title III — Americans with Disabilities Act requirements for public accommodations
  • Section 508 — Federal accessibility standards

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you experience any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days.
