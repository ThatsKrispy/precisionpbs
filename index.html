About Us

Why Choose
Precision?

For more than 45 years, Precision Paint & Body Bird Rd has been a trusted name in collision repair, serving Miami from the same location — a reputation built one vehicle at a time. As one of Miami-Dade's largest independent body shops, we offer complete collision services: frame and structural repairs to expert refinishing and mechanical restoration.

Our shop uses the premium Spies Hecker paint line, recognized and approved by luxury manufacturers including Mercedes-Benz and BMW. Every repair meets factory standards — and we back our paintwork with a lifetime guarantee.
